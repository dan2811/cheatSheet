How to link an external CSS stylesheet

Code Explanation
<link rel="stylesheet" href="filename.css"> Use a link tag to tell the browser what the filename is and what the relationship is.

Header Tags!

There are 6 header tags, they descend in importance as their "h" number increases.

Code Explanation
<h1> The most important in the heirarchy of the headings. Generally used for the main heading of the page.
<h2> The second most important in the heirarchy of the headings. Generally used for the first sub-heading of the page.
<h3> Less important that h2, more imprtant than h3.
<h4> Less important that h3, more imprtant than h5.
<h5> Less important that h4, more imprtant than h6.
<h6> The lowest on the heirarchy of heading tags.
